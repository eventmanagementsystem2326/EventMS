Event Management System ER Diagram

Professional presentation version of the database schema derived from supabase-schema.sql and app-settings-schema.sql. Primary keys are underlined. Foreign keys are italicized. Relationship diamonds show the business links between entities, while the dashed relationship indicates an application-level connection that is not enforced as a database foreign key.
